About Us

imadejptr is a game site that offers quality over quantity and clarity so you can get the games that YOU want to play. At imadejptr, here's what we strive for:

  • 1. Quality Games: Make sure that the games that people ask for are the best they possibly can be. This is done so we can stand out from other sites and have games that other sites would dream to have and what you guys would dream to play too.

  • 2. Clarity: The unbelievable number of game sites that have games that are advertised as one thing but are something that is not only not what was advertised, but wasn't even at a level for it to be tolerable to play. Here at imadejptr, we make sure that this site not only advertises what is on the site, but it has the games and quality to live up to what is advertised on the site.

  • 3. Ease of use: The best game sites are the ones where you just pick a game and play. We strive to make it as easy for anyone to play these games.

  • 4. Ad-free games: Do you ever hate it when you go on a game site, and you play a game you wanna play but right when you start playing it, an un-skippable 30-second ad pops up and blocks your whole screen? Well, that would be fine if they would stop the game, but 9 times out of 10 they never stop the game so every time you play that game, you're losing and there is 0 point in playing it because of the ads. Here on imadejptr, we make sure when we get the games you want, we RIP the ads out of the games themselves, making it easier, faster, and less of a hassle to play the games that you wanna play without getting those stupid pop-ups.

  • 5. Updated Weekly: Do you ever hate it when you go on a game site and you find a game that you wanna play, and you click on the said game and the game doesn't work or the link is dead and hasn't been updated since the dinosaurs were around? Well, we make sure that if there's something wrong with these games, we fix them and keep things accessible for everyone. So as long as this site is up, the updates will come.

  • 6. Community Input: Here, we love to hear from you guys on any improvements you want on the site, any games you want on the site, or just participating in a community event. We put our focus not on the money, but on the community at large because that's the people that are playing on the site and the only reason why the website is relevant.

  • 7. Unblocked Tested: Almost all the games are unblocked tested so if anyone is "doing their homework", they will be able to do so at school.

From a website that first started as an experiment to play games at school to pushing the boundaries of what's possible on a game site. This is imadejptr.
